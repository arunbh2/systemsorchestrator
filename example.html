Practical Action: The System Orchestrator

A neutral convener fostering collaboration for a sustainable & equitable Agriculture-Energy Nexus in India.

Core Functions

1. Convening Diverse Stakeholders
  • Leverage networks & reputation.
  • Bring together key players (farmers, energy providers, policy, NGOs, private sector, research).
  • Create platforms for dialogue & mutual understanding.
2. Facilitating Shared Vision & Agenda
  • Use participatory methods (workshops, dialogues, Theory U).
  • Collaboratively define challenges, priorities, and action plans.
3. Promoting Knowledge & Capacity
  • Act as a knowledge hub (collect, curate, disseminate).
  • Facilitate capacity building for stakeholders.
4. Supporting Pilots & Innovation
  • Support design, implementation, evaluation of innovative projects.
  • Provide technical aid, facilitate partnerships, help secure funding.
5. Advocating for Policy Change
  • Use ground insights to advocate for integrated state/national policies.
  • Engage policymakers, present evidence, build coalitions.
6. Fostering Market-Based Solutions
  • Facilitate development & scaling of market solutions.
  • Connect farmers & private sector, promote sustainable business models.

Guiding Principles & Approach

  • Practical Action's PMSD Approach
  • Systems Thinking
  • Participation
  • Facilitation
  • Gender Equality
  • Temporary, Catalytic Role
  • Empower Local Actors
  • Build Long-term Sustainability
